About K9 Solutions

Dedicated to dog training and pet care excellence

Who We Are

Our names are Scott and Tara Holliday, and we are the owners of K9 Solutions, Maryland branch. We have worked with dogs since the mid-1990s, and have helped many people with various forms of dog training ranging from basic obedience to advanced tracking and behavior modification. Wea unique perspective on working with dogs of all types and temperaments.

Our Story

In 2014, we decided to expand our focus into obedience training. Around that time, we acquired a search and rescue dog from our sister company in Ohio, which further deepened our involvement in working dog development. It was also during this period that we began working with Jeff Turner and started exploring the idea of building a business of our own.

After several years of hands-on training and mentorship with Jeff, we officially opened our own business in 2018.

Today, we train dogs in a wide range of disciplines, including obedience, man tracking, shed antler tracking, and blood tracking. We are certified balanced trainers who use positive, motivational-based methods that are proven to be effective in the canine world.

Above all, we are passionate about teaching owners how to train their own dogs. We believe every handler should become their dog's "own" trainer, building confidence, communication, and lasting results together.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dog training and pet care services that enhance the quality of life for both dogs and their families. We believe every dog deserves the opportunity to be their best self, and every owner deserves professional guidance and support.

Our training philosophy is rooted in patience, consistency, and clear communication, ensuring that both dogs and owners feel confident throughout the learning process. We focus on practical, real-world results that help dogs behave appropriately at home, in public, and in social environments. By combining proven training techniques with a personalized approach, we aim to build lasting habits that strengthen the bond between pets and their families.

Compassion

We treat every dog with the care and respect they deserve, understanding that they're valued family members.

Excellence

We continuously improve our methods and stay current with the latest in dog behavior science and training techniques.

Partnership

We work closely with owners to ensure training success both at our facility and in their homes.
