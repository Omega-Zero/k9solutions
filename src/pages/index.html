About Us

Our names are Scott and Tara Holliday, and we are the owners of K9 Solutions, Maryland branch. We have worked with dogs since the mid-1990s, and have helped many people with various forms of dog training ranging from basic obedience to advanced tracking and behavior modification. We have a unique perspective on working with dogs of all types and temperaments.

We're committed to providing the highest quality care for every dog that comes through our doors. Your pet's safety, happiness, and well-being are our top priorities.
